About Us

Who we are and what drives us

Telecom Infrastructure Specialists

Nitlinc Networking OPC Pvt Ltd is a specialized telecom infrastructure company incorporated in 2025, headquartered in Rewa, Madhya Pradesh. We deliver end-to-end commissioning, installation, and maintenance of telecom equipment across India.

We are a trusted partner of ECI Telecom and work with all major Indian operators including Airtel, Jio, Vi (Vodafone Idea), BSNL, and Tata Communications. Our team has hands-on expertise with OTN/SDH platforms, fiber optics, LCT-based configuration, and live network O&M.

Our operations span across Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, and other states, ensuring connectivity reaches even the most remote locations of India.
